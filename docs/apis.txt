contest endpoints

root /api/contests-app/

- /contests/ongoing/: віддає slug of ongoing contest

- /contests/<slug>/ - контест
- /contests/ - всі контести

Endpoints to solve contest:
- /solve/create/?discipline=<id> - create solve (that user just solved)
- /solve/<id>/submit/ - submit solve

- /scramble/current/?discipline=<slug>
- /get-all-solve-of-contest-in-discipline/

що вопинно бути у стейт?
